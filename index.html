The opportunity

Most employers have never heard of this

Every payroll cycle, you pay 7.65% FICA on each W2 employee's wages. A Section 125 Wellness Cafeteria Plan allows employees to receive pre-tax wellness benefits — which reduces the wages subject to FICA.

The result: lower employer FICA liability, lower workers comp premiums in most states, and a real benefit your employees can use.

This isn't a loophole. It's been in the IRS code for decades. Most employers just don't know it exists.

FAQ

Common questions

Is this really IRS-compliant?

Yes. Section 125 has been part of the IRS tax code since 1978. Our plan has been independently reviewed by Hitesman Law, KCP Tax Advisory Group, and CBIZ Advisors — three separate legal and tax authorities.

What does it cost to implement?

There is no net cost to the employer. The savings from reduced FICA and workers comp premiums typically exceed any administrative fees.

What benefits do employees receive?

Employees gain access to wellness benefits including telehealth, prescription discounts, mental health counseling, and preventive care — at no cost to them.

How long does implementation take?

Most employers are fully enrolled within 2–4 weeks of signing.

Do I need to change my existing health insurance?

No. The Wellness 125 plan works alongside your existing group health coverage. There are no changes required to your current benefits.
